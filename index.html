About Us

We are Team Coding aka Team C.


Our idea for this website was to provide a solution to the countless hours lost due to browsing for a good movie to watch. With so many streaming devices we spend a lot of our time browsing through content finding the right movie to watch. So our team came up with a simple solution. Based on your mood, we will narrow down your seach and give you a list of movies for you to choose. This will be saving valuable screen time in which you can use to prepare yourself a snack instead. Also as a bonus we provide you a meal or snack to go along with your movie, making your movie night more enjoyable.
